Best Ramen Recipe

This recipe will show you everything you will need and how to cook this delicious meal!

  • Ramen noodles
  • chicken dashi
  • shiitake muschrooms
  • chashu
  • green onion

Steps

  1. Once water is boiling, add noodles
  2. When noodles have finished cooking, add ingrediants
  3. Turn off heat, let cool for a minutes, and enjoy!
