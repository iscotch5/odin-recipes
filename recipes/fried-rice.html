Fried Rice Recipe

Best Lasagna Recipe

This recipe will show you everything you will need and how to cook this delicious meal!

  • Rice
  • Mushrooms
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Soy Sauce
  • Carrot/pea mixture

Steps

  1. Cook rice for about 20 minutes
  2. Saute mushrooms for 5 minutes, set aside
  3. Cook rice with garlic, ginger, and soy sauce for 2 minutes
  4. Add mushrooms and mixed veggies and cook for 5 minutes
  5. Serve and enjoy!
